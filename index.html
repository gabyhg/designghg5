    About

    "I firmly believe that the most important part
    is to understand and empathize with
    the target audience to drive the design process."

    -Gaby Huerta-Golbad

    Gaby is a freelance designer based in San Francisco, California. She works for companies, institutions, and individuals in the United States and Mexico. She uses her knowledge of UI/UX design to help her clients increase their revenues as they sell and promote their products and services. She believes a well-developed design product can emotionally connect with potential customers who can convert into daily users. As a passionate designer, she keeps up-to-date on the newest trends, software, and technologies. Gaby stays informed by actively researching, reading, and studying everything related to the design industry. She has studied graphic design, graphic production, digital advertising, brand strategy and currently studying a BFA in Web Design and New Media at the Academy of Art University.
