    Project Information

  • Name: Serene

  • Category: Walking Meditation App

  • Date: 2018

  • view prototype

The purpose of this app is to provide meditation tracks that users can play while they are walking. This smartphone app will remind the user to listen to meditation tracks everyday making sure that users stick to a new daily routine. The idea is to keep the user in the present and focused. This walking meditation app will emphasize not only meditation but also focus, inspiration, and relaxation. It will help users to feel more at peace.
