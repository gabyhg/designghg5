    Project Information

  • Name: San Carlos Art & Wine Faire

  • Category: Integrated Campaign

  • Date: 2019

  • prototype

San Carlos Art & Wine Faire is a festival in the Bay Area of San Francisco. It takes place in October every year. The goal is to project this event as a vibrant and festive faire. Earthy color palette is used to evoke the time of the year when this event takes place. The objective of creating a hand-lettered for the logotype is to represent the fine artists and artisans that participate. The design composition conveys the exciting activities and live entertainment as well as international food and premium wines that people can enjoy.
