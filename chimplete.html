    Project Information

  • Name: Chimplete

  • Category: Game Educational App

  • Date: 2019

  • chimplete.com

Discover San Francisco with Chimplete is an educational 2D video game. The player will find clues, solve puzzles and discover hidden treasures to unlock areas in San Francisco. The goal is to help Chimplete to find his friend Koko, who was adopted by a family that lives in San Francisco. It is a fun and entertaining touchscreen video game with colorful graphics.
